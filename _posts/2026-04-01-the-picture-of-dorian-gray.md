This is one of the classics, and I remember Mdm Jamilah Lajam praising this book back in English class in Year Five. After reading it, I cannot for the life of me understand why it is a classic. The only thing that I thought was mildly interesting was the way Oscar Wilde uses Lord Henry to portray controversial ideas in a very artistic and convincing manner. He does have quotable lines, but I'm not here to do a book analysis, so that's that.
