---
title: "The Old Man and the Sea"
author: "Ernest Hemingway"
rating: 4
---

This book made me feel like I should really up my game when it comes to masculinity. This old man is so hardwired on his one goal of catching a big fish that I think he must definitely have some sort of autism. Nevertheless, his unwavering spirit when it comes to the game of attrition between him and the big fish is very motivational. I think it helps that he's an old man, so his determination seems very counterintuitive to his old age, which makes the book all the more inspiring. The way Ernest Hemingway describes fishing makes me want to get out on a boat and fish as well. Amen to that.
