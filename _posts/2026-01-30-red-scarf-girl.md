---
title: "Red Scarf Girl"
author: "Ji-Li Jiang"
rating: 3
---

I don't have much to say about this book other than the fact that it taught me more about the cultural revolution led by chairman Mao, the fourolds, and that the goal of the cultural revolution was to enforce communist ideals. It's interesting how much the Chinese citizens themselves suffered from the cultural revolution, and yet they somehow still understand and appreciate the cultural revolution that had taken so much from them, all in the name of bettering society in the eyes of chairman Mao.
