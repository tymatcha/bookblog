---
title: "A Life on Our Planet"
author: "David Attenborough"
rating: 3
---

This book filled me with a deep love for the planet and made my heart hurt every time I think about how it is being destroyed by humankind — whether it's throwing waste into the ocean, dumping oil, killing marine animals through whale hunting, coral reefs dying, the Amazon rainforest being burned away, or animals going into extinction due to human hunting and the clearing of timber. It just made me feel really bad about what we do as a byproduct of our development as a species.
