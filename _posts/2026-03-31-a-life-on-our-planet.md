---
title: "A Life on Our Planet"
author: "David Attenborough"
rating: 3
---
This book filled me with deep love for the planet and made my heart hurt every time I think about how it is being destroyed by humankind when we throw away waste into the ocean, dump oil into the ocean, kill marine animals through the process of whale hunting, or coral reefs dying, or the Amazon rainforest being burned away, or animals going into extinction due to human hunting or the burning of timber. This just made me feel really bad about what we do as a byproduct of our development as a species.
