---
title: "Orbital"
author: "Samantha Harvey"
rating: 4
---

This book is written from the perspective of astronauts in space orbiting around the Earth. It offers a unique perspective on how international affairs and international borders are all just man-made constructs. The descriptive language used to highlight the diverse spectrum of colours that paint our Earth makes it such a pleasant read. I think the part of the book about the Japanese girl and her mum who recently passed away was written very well. I can't really remember what it was about, but I remember being very touched.
