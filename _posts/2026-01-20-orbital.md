---
title: "Orbital"
author: "Samantha Harvey"
rating: 4
---

This book is written from the perspective of astronauts in space orbiting around the Earth. It shares a unique perspective on how international affairs, international borders are all just man-made constructs. The descriptive language used to highlight the diverse spectrum of colors that paint our earth makes it such a pleasant read. I think the part of the book about the Japanese girl and her mom who recently passed away, was written very well. I can't really remember what it was about, but I remember being very touched.
