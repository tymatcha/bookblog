---
title: "Big Little Lies"
author: "Liane Moriarty"
rating: 4
---

This book feels like a book created towards mothers in the way that it explores the hardships of motherhood so deeply. The way that the characters are portrait in this book is so well done, every character feels three dimensional, and it made me care for each and every single one of them. The male characters on the other hand are very two dimensional, and I don't know if this is a intentional decision made by the author. The plot twist was pretty crazy as well. I didn't expect who it was to die. The read at the book foreshadows the death, very intentionally through the countdown such as "four weeks before trivia night", and the periodic insertion of the manuscript of a detectives investigation is so cool.
