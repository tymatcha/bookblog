title: "Frankenstein"
author: "Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley"
rating: 4
---

This book being one of the most read classics definitely did not disappoint. I like the way that the narrative structure follows letters being sent between the sailor and his loved one. Or reading the book I felt so much pity for the ghoul as he initially had good intentions and hope to be accepted by society, and even after proving himself to be docile due to his sheer ugliness, he was shunned by the family which he had observed from a distance and learnt English from. If I'm not wrong, the book is situated somewhere in Switzerland so I recognizing some of the landmarks mentioned like the city of Geneva. I think the book touches on deeper point than I can comprehend so all I can say about this book was that it made me feel pity for the ghoul and it made me contemplate about how much appearances matter in life. 

If I'm being honest, the biggest thing that I learned was that Frankenstein is not the name of the monster, but instead of the doctor that made it.
