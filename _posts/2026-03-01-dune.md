---
title: "Dune"
author: "Frank Herbert"
rating: 2
---

This book uses so many weird terms that are completely unfamiliar to anybody reading it, because the author decided to use terms that he himself created to describe his fictional world. You have to use a lot of imagination to picture the world-building elements. I think it's better to watch the movie than to read this book. I don't think it was a very good book. Kind of lame, and I don't understand why it's known as one of the founding fathers of sci-fi novels.
