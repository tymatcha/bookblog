title: "Abroad in Japan"
author: "Chris Broad"
rating: 5
---

I love this guy's YouTube channel and I love the language that he uses in his documentary-like videos. And I love the topic that he talks about which is staying abroad in Japan as he gets through life as a English teacher in a Japanese school somewhere north of Japan in the nasty winter cold. I think it's so interesting that he moved to Japan without much love for or inspiration from anime. I would've thought that the only reason for white people moving to Japan would be because they like to watch anime. I thoroughly enjoyed this read because his writing style feels very personal, and I just love Japan and it's culture so naturally, I enjoyed reading about it.
