---
title: "Strange Houses"
author: "Uketsu"
rating: 2
---

I think I read this book in a day. It's really short and it's captivating more than anything else. Can't say much about the language used in the book because it's mostly simple English, and I just read it for good fun. I do remember that the ending of the book fell flat, and I'm not sure if this was intentional by the writer or not, but I was highly dissatisfied with it. Also, the whole family history thing was such a mess and it was hard to keep track of the big family tree.
